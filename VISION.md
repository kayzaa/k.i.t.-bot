## The Three Levels of Autonomy

### Level 1: Assistant Mode
- K.I.T. suggests, human approves
- Full transparency, full control
- Good for learning and building trust

### Level 2: Co-Pilot Mode
- Small actions: autonomous
- Large actions: ask for approval
- Configurable thresholds

### Level 3: Full Autopilot
- K.I.T. handles everything
- Human gets daily reports
- Emergency alerts only
- **This is the goal.**
